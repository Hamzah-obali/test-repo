About

The first deli was made in 1888 named iceland brothers and it was established on ludlow street
in New York's lower east side by the Iceland brothers. On the arrival of Willy Katz in 1903 the name
was changed to Iceland and Kat'z. In 1910 Will's cousin Benny joined Willy in buying out the place form
the Iceland brothers. The store has been passed down now from generation to generation for 100 years.

A lot of people took the idea and Now there are hundreds of delis open throughout NYC.
